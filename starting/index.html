Colours

This section shows the colours and corresponding #HEXA code.

Standard Blue Colour.

Blue #HEXA = #0000ff. This is the standard blue colour for our styling.

Darker Blue Shades.

Blue #HEXA = #0000e6. This is the dark blue colour.

Blue #HEXA = #0000cc. This is a darker shade of blue.

Blue #HEXA = #0000b3. This is the darkest shade of blue.

Lighter Blue Shades.

Blue #HEXA = #3333ff. This is a light blue colour.

Blue #HEXA = #4d4dff. This is a lighter shade of blue.

Blue #HEXA = #6666ff. This is a lightest shade of blue.
